About Us

Narmbool is a 2,000 hectare property owned and operated by Sovereign Hill. It is an example of sustainable farming in a diverse and fragile ecosystem and is offered to schools as a camp venue. Narmbool was donated to Sovereign Hill in 2000 for the express purpose of developing the property as an educational resource.
